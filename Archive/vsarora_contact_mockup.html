📋 Designer Notes — Contact Us Page
Short, direct, and friction-free. 5 sections max. Every element is designed to lower the hesitation to reach out.
🔴 Hero Rewrite
Remove "Call us 24/7" framing. Replace with: "Let's protect your brand. It starts with a free call." Plus the reassuring sub: "No legal jargon. No obligation." This directly removes the #1 reason people don't contact law firms online.
🟡 3 Contact Methods
WhatsApp (green card, always first — this is how most Indian SME clients prefer contact), Call (dark card), Book a Slot (gold card). Each shows a response time. Giving choices reduces abandonment — if they won't call, they'll WhatsApp.
🔵 Form Improvements
Add service dropdown (Trademark / Patent / Copyright / Company Law / Litigation / Other). This pre-qualifies the lead so VS Arora knows what they're dealing with before the call. Textarea is optional — reduces form friction.
🟢 Map + Office Details
Keep the Google Maps embed — it signals physical presence. Add a clean "Office Information" card with address, phone, email, hours. Verify the Google Business listing matches this address before launch.
🔴 Fix: Wrong Phone
The current page shows "869-5414-5" — a template placeholder that was never removed. Replace with +91 (033) 6291807816 everywhere on the page. This is causing lost leads every single day until fixed.
🟡 Fix: Remove "24/7"
Replace the false "24/7" claim with an honest response time statement: "We respond within 2 hours during office hours." Honest promises build more trust than inflated ones — especially for a boutique firm where clients want a real person, not a bot.
